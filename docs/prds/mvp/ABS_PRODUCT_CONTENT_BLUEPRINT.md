# Asset Business Solutions (ABS)
## Product Content Blueprint v1.0

Purpose:
Define ALL content assets required to build the ABS digital showroom.

This includes:
- Images
- Copy blocks
- Product data
- Visual demonstrations
- SaaS explanations

This document guides:
- photographers
- designers
- developers
- AI content generation
- marketing teams

---

# 1. Content Philosophy

ABS content must:

SHOW capability before EXPLAINING it.

Priority order:

1. Visual proof
2. Outcome statement
3. Supporting explanation
4. Technical detail

Avoid long marketing paragraphs.

---

- PNG fallback
