# Asset Business Solutions (ABS)

## Launch Readiness Matrix

Purpose:
Provide a single operational view of all work required to launch:

- Arcplus Asset Management System
- ABS Digital Product Website
- Hardware + Services Self-Service Experience

This matrix ensures nothing critical is missed before launch.

---

# 1. Launch Definition

Launch means the following must be possible:

Users can:

- Visit the ABS website
- Understand products and services
- Start an Arcplus free trial
- Compare subscription plans
- Request quotes for scanners or tags
- Register for training
- Contact ABS when necessary

Internal team can:

- Receive quote requests
- Manage trials
- Process subscriptions
- Respond to clients

# 8. Launch Gate Checklist

Before announcing launch:

✔ Website loads under 2 seconds  
✔ Free trial creates working account  
✔ RFQ submissions reach ABS inbox  
✔ Pricing page displays correctly  
✔ Mobile site works smoothly  
✔ Admin dashboard operational  

---

# 9. Soft Launch Strategy

Recommended approach:

Phase 1 — Internal Test  
Invite 5–10 trusted organizations.

Phase 2 — Quiet Launch  
Website live but not heavily promoted.

Phase 3 — Public Launch  
Announce Arcplus officially.
